Our Story

Founded in 2010, Oral Mint Dentistry began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional dental care in a comfortable, welcoming environment. What started as a small practice has grown into one of the most trusted dental clinics in the area.

Our founder, Dr. Sarah Mitchell, envisioned a dental practice where patients would never feel anxious about their visits. Over the years, we've built a team of skilled professionals who share this vision and are passionate about transforming smiles.

Today, we continue to invest in the latest dental technologies and techniques, ensuring our patients receive the highest standard of care. But what truly sets us apart is our commitment to building lasting relationships with every patient who walks through our doors.
